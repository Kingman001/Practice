The Summary of the Life of Jesus Christ

  • 5 B.C - Jesus born in Bethlehem.
  • 5 B.C - Visit of the Maggi from the east.
  • 5 B.C - Jesus dedicated in the temple.
  • 5 B.C - Jesus taken to Egypt.
  • AD 12 - Jesus stayed back after the Passover at the Jerusalem temple, discussing with the teachers of the law.
  • AD 30 - Jesus was baptized by John the Baptist at Jordan river.
  • AD 30 - Jesus starts his public ministry.
  • AD 33 - Jesus was crucified on the cross in Jerusalem.
  • AD 33 - The resurrection of Jesus Christ.
  • AD 33 - Jesus' ascension to Heaven.
